Yes. Sanskar International School, Hajipur is officially affiliated to the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE). It follows the CBSE curriculum from Nursery through Class XII (10+2).

Play Class welcomes children from 1.5–2.5 years — the earliest start to a child's learning journey. For Nursery, children should be 3–4 years by 31st March of the admission year. For KG (LKG/UKG), 4–5 years. Please confirm exact cut-off dates with the school office.

School hours are 8:00 AM to 3:00 PM, Monday through Saturday. The campus is closed on Sundays and public holidays.

The school provides bus service covering Hajipur city, Lalganj, Jandaha, and Jadua. Transport routes are updated annually. Contact the school office at +91 7707090111 to confirm pick-up points for your specific area.

The entire campus is equipped with 24×7 CCTV surveillance. The school maintains a bully-free, peaceful environment with trained and caring staff ensuring complete safety and peace of mind for parents.

Sanskar International School uniquely blends modern education with strong Indian values. We offer a perfect balance of academic excellence, discipline, moral values, and modern teaching methods — including activity-based learning, NEP 2020 curriculum, smart classrooms, and holistic personality development in a safe, inspiring environment.

We offer sports (cricket, football, badminton, kabaddi, chess, yoga), clubs (debate, quiz, art & craft, music, dance), and annual events including Annual Day, Sports Day, Cultural Fest, and educational tours to ancient historical sites.
